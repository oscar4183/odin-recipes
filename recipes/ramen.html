Ramen Noodles

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth

Ingredients

  • package of two servings of noodles
  • flavor packet
  • half cup of water
  1. Take the water and heat it up in a medium sized bowl until it begins boiling.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the dried noodles.
  3. Let the noodles cook for three minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove from heat, and in a bowl add a quarter cup of the pasta water.
  5. Drain the rest of the noodles.
  6. Add noodles to bowl along with the contents of the flavor packet.
  7. Stir thoroughly for one minute.
  8. Enjoy!
